FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

1. Do I have to necessarily register to shop at KMM?

No, you can place the order using Guest Checkout option without registration.

2. Can I have multiple registrations?

No need. Each email address, shipping address and contact phone number can only be associated with one account.

3. What are the modes of payment?

We have cash on delivery (COD), PAYTM, Google Pay and Card payment options available. You can make the payment during the time of delivery at your doorstep.

4. Are there any other charges or taxes in addition to the price shown?

All taxes are included in the MRP of products. There are no additional taxes added to your order.

5. When will I receive my order?

Once you are done selecting your products and click on checkout you will find a pop up on screen showing your date of delivery. Depending on your pin-code, your delivery date is decided. We try to deliver your items within 3 working days. For details of the delivery schedule in your area, kindly check the delivery chart provided. For first time users, our customer care executive will contact you to get your full address and other relevant information.

6. How is the delivery done? Is delivery available across Pune?

We have a dedicated team of delivery personnel and a fleet of vehicles operating across our delivery area which ensures timely and accurate delivery to our customers. Currently we do not deliver all over Pune. Orders outside delivery area are not processed. For details of the delivery schedule and areas of devices, kindly check the delivery chart provided. If you think your location needs to be added, then kindly mail us at keralaminimart@gmail.com or Whatsapp us at 7218068071.

7. How much are the delivery charges?

Delivery is free for orders of Rs. 250.00 and above. Orders below Rs. 250.00 will be processed and charged Rs 30/-.

8. What are the delivery timings?

Monday to Saturday from 2.00 PM to 10.00 PM (Sunday holiday) If customer needs specific slot time for your convenience, you can contact our customer care 7218068071.

9. I'm not finding a product or I'd like to suggest some products. Whom do I contact?

If you are unable to find a product or brand that you would like to shop for, please call our customer care number or write to us at keralaminimart@gmail.com or you can add to the Wishlist and we will try our best to make the product available for you.

10. How & where I can give my feedback?

We always welcome feedback, both positive and negative from all our customers. Please feel free to write to us @ keralaminimart@gmail.com, or call us on our customer care number and we will do our best to incorporate your suggestions into our system.

11. How do I contact customer service?

Our customer service team is available throughout the week except Sunday from 9 am to 7 pm can be reached at 7218068071 or via email @ keralaminimart@gmail.com

12. I found some products defective, what's your return/replacement policy?

In case you find any delivered product defective/damaged/not satisfied, you can report to the delivery person during the delivery of your order. We will take back the product and will adjust the bill. We maintain an absolute "no questions asked" policy at the time of delivery. If you are not satisfied with the freshness or quality of the products, please return the items to the delivery executive at time of delivery. Please note that we will not accept the return of the products which are in opened condition or refrigerated items.
